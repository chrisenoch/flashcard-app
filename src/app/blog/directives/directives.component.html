Directives with RXJS to add timers

  • The timers are for showing/hiding content after a certain amount of time.
  • The timers pause on screen resize and resume after resize has finished.
  • You can cancel, pause or resume the timers.
  • Refresh the screen to see them in action.
  • After cancelling, you must refresh the page because once they have been cancelled, they cannot be resumed.
